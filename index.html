Firm Overview

Mawalkar & Co. is an independent advisory firm providing strategic guidance to investors and business owners. The firm specializes in identifying and evaluating acquisition opportunities, conducting market and industry research, and supporting the early stages of strategic transactions. Combining rigorous financial analysis with advanced data and AI-driven insights, Mawalkar & Co. delivers clear, disciplined perspectives on industries, companies, and trends. The firm’s services help clients make informed decisions, streamline opportunity assessment, and approach transactions with confidence and clarity.

Services

Buy-Side Advisory

Mawalkar & Co. provides buy-side advisory services to investors and strategic acquirers seeking to identify and evaluate potential acquisition targets. We help clients navigate the early stages of the investment process by conducting market research, evaluating strategic fit, and providing financial and operational insights. Our approach is disciplined and tailored to each client’s objectives, allowing investors to make informed decisions with confidence while preserving operational focus and confidentiality.

Data & AI-Driven Research

Leveraging advanced data analysis and AI-driven methodologies, Mawalkar & Co. delivers enhanced insights on industries, competitors, and financial performance. Our tools allow clients to uncover trends, identify emerging opportunities, and generate proprietary intelligence that supports strategic decision-making. This approach complements traditional advisory services, providing a deeper and more systematic perspective to support transaction evaluation and investment planning.

Principal

Krish Mawalkar is the founder and principal of Mawalkar & Co., an independent advisory firm devoted to the interests of investors and business proprietors. Mr. Mawalkar previously co-founded Arlington Consulting Group & Associates LLC, a strategy consultancy serving family and founder-led enterprises. His prior experience includes engagements at the Federal Reserve, encompassing mergers & acquisitions and research initiatives in artificial intelligence, as well as at J.P. Morgan Chase in analytical operations, U.S. Bank, and a Pittsburgh-based investment banking practice. Mr. Mawalkar is also a Research Assistant at Carnegie Mellon University’s Tepper School of Business, contributing to projects in the Accounting AI Research Lab and the Center for Behavioral and Decision Research.
